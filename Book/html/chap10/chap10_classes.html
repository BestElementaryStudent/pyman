Classes¶

No discussion of Python, scientific or otherwise, would be complete without a discussion of Python classes. Classes are the heart of what’s known as “object-oriented programming” or OOP. Our discussion of classes here is far from complete. In fact, it’s barely an introduction. Nevertheless, this chapter introduces classes, their basic features, and some of the ways they are used in scientific programming.
